Regular Evaluation

To maintain high-quality education standards, we conduct regular evaluations and provide constructive feedback to franchisees.

Evaluation Includes:

  • Teacher performance and classroom practices.
  • Student progress tracking and assessments.
  • Infrastructure and facility maintenance checks.
  • Feedback sessions with parents and community.
Goal: Continuous improvement ensures every child gets the best possible education in a joyful environment.
