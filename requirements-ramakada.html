Ramakada Method

Ramakada is our unique approach to joyful learning, where children learn through stories, games, and hands-on activities instead of rote memorization.

Key Features:

  • Storytelling sessions for moral and value-based learning.
  • Play-based activities to enhance creativity.
  • Group games to build teamwork and confidence.
  • Hands-on craft and experiments for experiential learning.
Did you know? Research shows that play-based learning improves problem-solving skills and creativity among children.
